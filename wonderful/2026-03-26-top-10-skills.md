## Top 10 Skills

### 1. **Architecture Designer** ⭐⭐⭐
- Designs and validates system architectures; generates diagrams, decision matrices, trade-offs
- **Use case:** Your daily bread—SoftTech architecture decisions, AI integration patterns

### 2. **Architecture Review**
- Critiques existing systems for scalability, security, performance; suggests improvements
- **Use case:** Legacy system audit, SoftTech performance baseline analysis

### 3. **AI Tech Lead**
- Guides AI strategy, team structure, model selection, implementation roadmaps
- **Use case:** Leading your AI team, defining İş Bankası's AI architecture strategy

### 4. **Performance Optimization**
- Identifies bottlenecks, suggests tuning, measures impact
- **Use case:** Your SoftTech optimization focus—pinpoint performance issues fast

### 5. **Decision Engine**
- Structures complex decisions with weighted criteria, scenarios, risk analysis
- **Use case:** Strategic decisions on AI adoption, tool selection, resource allocation

### 6. **SQL Query Optimizer**
- Analyzes database queries, suggests indexes, rewrites for speed
- **Use case:** SoftTech database layer optimization (if SQL-heavy)

### 7. **McKinsey Research**
- Gathers competitive intelligence, market trends, best practices for exec briefings
- **Use case:** Building context for executive presentations on AI strategy

### 8. **AI Presentation Maker**
- Generates slide decks from raw notes/data; layouts optimized for exec audiences
- **Use case:** Convert your strategy memos into polished decks for İş Bankası leadership

### 9. **Agent Orchestration (Multi-Agent Optimize)**
- Designs multi-agent workflows; optimizes communication, load-balancing, coordination
- **Use case:** If building AI agent teams (highly relevant for your AI strategy work)

### 10. **Financial Analyst**
- ROI analysis, cost modeling, budget impact forecasting
- **Use case:** Present AI investment business case to executives with hard numbers
